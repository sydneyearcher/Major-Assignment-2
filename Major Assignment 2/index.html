Kuromi

Kuromi is Melody's rival, another bunny. Kuromi dons a black jester hat with a pink skull and a little black devils hair. Her birthday is on Halloween (October 31st) and her name trasnlates to black beauty in English. Kuromi is popular among goth, punk, and scene crowds because of her appearance. Her character enjoys writting in her diary and cooking. She loves romantic short stories, shallots and meat and her favorite colors are black and hot pink. She also loves makinf mischief. She is usually described as a villian a punk and a tomboy.

Melody

Mai Merodi, also known as My Melody in english and usually shortened as Melody is known as Hello Kitty's best friend. Her character is inspired by Little Red Riding Hood hence her little pink hood. Her place of birth is the forest of Mariland and is a rabbit. She loves to back, and enjoys Almond poundcake and her famed hood that her grandmother made for her. She is descirbed as a very honest, good natured and kind rabbit. She has one sibling named Rythm another sanrio character.

Bad Badtz-Maru

Bad Badtz-Maru is a spunky little penguin with spiky hair. Unlike like other Sanrio characters he is actually known for his attitude. Bad Badtz-Maru is also one of the only characters that are makerted to all genders. He was born on April 1st, A.K.A April Fool's Day! He also love to collect pictures of movie stars. Some of his skills are listed as "pretending" to play the piano and escaping (?). Fun fact: Bad Badtz-Maru has a pet alligator and is friends with another Sanrio character Pandaba.

Chococat

Chococat is described as a sweet chocolate colored kitty, who was born in Chocotown Colorado. His whiskers are said to pick up information like antennae so he is often the first to know things. He was born May 10 and is friends with Cookie-Bau, Jelly Bean, and Nutz the hamster. Fun fact! In Choco's wiki it states that his parents Rory and Catrina are currently divorced. He is also refered to as Hello Kitty's counterpart in the official Sanrio Wiki Fandom.
